1. Backlog Intelligence

As a Product Owner, I want the AI to analyze backlog items for completeness and prioritization, so that I can ensure readiness for sprint planning1.
As a Business Analyst, I want the AI to synthesize customer feedback into backlog insights, so that we can build what users actually need1.
2. Story Creation & Refinement

As a Scrum Master, I want the AI assistant to generate user stories from raw requirements or meeting transcripts, so that I can accelerate backlog grooming and maintain traceability to Epics and OKRs1.
As a Product Owner, I want the assistant to suggest acceptance criteria for each user story, so that stories are testable and ready for development1.
3. Meeting Intelligence

As a Scrum Master, I want the AI to attend agile ceremonies and identify impediments or blockers, so that I can proactively resolve them1.
As a team member, I want the AI to summarize key takeaways and action items from sprint reviews and planning sessions, so that nothing is missed1.
4. Team Sentiment & Wellness

As a Scrum Master, I want the AI to monitor team sentiment through chat and meeting tone analysis, so that I can detect burnout or disengagement early2.
5. Ceremony Facilitation

As a Scrum Master, I want the AI to facilitate daily standups by summarizing blockers, progress, and priorities, so that team members stay aligned without manual prep.
As a team member, I want the AI to transcribe retrospectives and generate structured summaries, so that we can track improvements over time1.
6. Flow Metrics & Coaching Insights

As a Delivery Lead, I want the AI to aggregate flow metrics (e.g., cycle time, throughput), so that I can identify coaching opportunities and delivery bottlenecks1.
As an Agile Coach, I want the AI to surface patterns in team behavior and suggest interventions, so that I can tailor my coaching approach1.
